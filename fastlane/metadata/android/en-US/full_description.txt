OpenScanner is a free and open-source document scanner.

Point your camera at a page: OpenScanner finds the four corners in real time, crops the page flat when you capture, and applies a filter that makes text crisp black on a clean white background — like a real scanner, not a photo.

Features:

- Live edge detection with a highlighted outline in the viewfinder
- Automatic crop and perspective correction on capture
- Magic filter: vivid black text on clean white, with shadows removed
- More filters: color enhance, grayscale, original
- Multi-page documents, exported and shared as PDF
- Import existing photos from your gallery
- Manual corner adjustment with a magnifier

Private by design:

- 100% offline — the app has no network access at all
- No ads, no watermarks, no account, no tracking
- Free software under the GPL-3.0 license
